Hollow Knight: Silksong is the long-awaited sequel to the award-winning Hollow Knight. This time, you take control of Hornet, princess-protector of Hallownest, as she journeys to a vast new kingdom haunted by silk and song.

Silksong expands on the fast-paced, challenging gameplay of the original, introducing new tools, quests, and over 150 brand-new enemies. With stunning environments, epic boss battles, and Hornet's acrobatic combat style, Silksong is both a continuation and a fresh experience for fans of the first game.
