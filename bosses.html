Discover 10 of the greatest bosses in Hollow Knight.

10.False Knight

A massive, ancient knight who attacks with his huge hammer. The fight focuses on taking hits carefully and finding attack opportunities, as his movements can be unpredictable. Defeating him requires patience and precise timing.

9.Traitor Lord

A fierce and agile warrior who betrayed his tribe. He attacks with relentless slashes and wide area strikes, forcing quick reactions. The battle demands sharp dodging and constant awareness to survive his speed and power.

8.White Defender

A nimble knight clad in white armor, guarding his territory with fast sword strikes and sudden lunges. The fight emphasizes timing and precise movement to avoid his rapid attacks and find openings to counter.

7.Soul Master

A powerful sorcerer who wields devastating soul-based attacks. He teleports and casts energy projectiles, requiring careful dodging and strategic timing to strike safely.

6.Lost Kin

A corrupted, faster version of the Hollow Knight, striking with swift, relentless attacks. The battle tests quick reflexes and precise dodging to survive its aggressive assault.

5.Hornet

A skilled and agile warrior who attacks with fast, precise needle strikes. The fight requires quick reflexes and careful timing to dodge her combos and counterattack effectively.

4.Radiance

A powerful, divine being that wields blinding light and energy attacks. The battle demands perfect dodging and endurance, as her attacks cover large areas and come in relentless waves.

3.Mantis Lords

A trio of skilled, fast-moving warriors who attack with precise sword strikes and coordinated combos. The fight tests timing, reflexes, and pattern recognition to defeat them efficiently

2.Pure Vessel

A highly agile and deadly warrior, the Pure Vessel strikes with swift, relentless attacks and fluid movements. Defeating it requires perfect timing, precise dodging, and endurance.

1. Nightmare King Grimm

A fiery, nightmarish warrior who attacks with fast, explosive strikes and acrobatic maneuvers. The fight demands quick reflexes, precise dodging, and relentless focus to survive his onslaught.
